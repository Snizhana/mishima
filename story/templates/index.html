The Life and Death of

Yukio Mishima

January 14, 1925 — November 25, 1970

Japanese author, poet, playwright, actor, and film director. Mishima is considered one of the most important Japanese authors of the 20th century, he was nominated three times for the Nobel Prize in Literature. He is remembered for his ritual suicide by seppuku after a failed coup d'état attempt, known as the «Mishima Incident».
